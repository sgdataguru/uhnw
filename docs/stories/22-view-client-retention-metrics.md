# User Story: 22 - View Client Retention and Attrition Metrics

**As a** Managing Partner,
**I want** to view a dedicated client retention and attrition metrics dashboard,
**so that** I can monitor client loyalty and identify retention issues early.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Dashboard displays overall retention rate with trend
* Attrition breakdown by reason, segment, and time period
* Early warning indicators for at-risk clients
* Comparison of retention across RMs and regions
* Cohort analysis showing retention over client lifetime
* Integration with churn risk scores
* Drill-down to individual client level

## Notes

* Additional elements: "Client retention/attrition metrics dashboard"
* Critical for understanding client loyalty in UHNW segment
* Helps identify systemic issues vs. isolated cases
