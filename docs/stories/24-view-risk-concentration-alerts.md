# User Story: 24 - View Risk Concentration Alerts

**As a** Relationship Manager,
**I want** to receive alerts about portfolio and regulatory risk concentrations,
**so that** I can proactively address potential compliance or risk issues.

## Acceptance Criteria

* System monitors portfolio concentrations across clients
* Alerts generated when concentration thresholds are exceeded
* Risk types include: sector concentration, single stock exposure, regulatory limits
* Alerts display affected client, risk type, and severity
* Recommended actions provided with each alert
* Integration with compliance and regulatory requirements
* Historical view of concentration trends

## Notes

* Additional elements: "Risk concentration alerts (portfolio & regulatory)"
* Critical for UHNW segment due to large position sizes
* Proactive alerting prevents compliance issues
