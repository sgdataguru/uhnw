# User Story: 18 - View Conversion Rates and Churn Risk Scoring

**As a** Managing Partner,
**I want** to see conversion rates and churn risk scores for prospects and clients,
**so that** I can monitor pipeline effectiveness and proactively address retention risks.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Dashboard displays overall conversion rates from lead to client
* Conversion funnel visualization with stage-by-stage metrics
* Churn risk score displayed for each existing client
* Risk scoring includes factors and explanation
* Alerts for high-risk clients requiring attention
* Historical trends for conversion and churn metrics
* Filterable by RM, region, or client segment

## Notes

* Page 1 highlights: "Conversion rates with churn risk scoring"
* Helps identify both acquisition effectiveness and retention risks
* Churn prevention is as important as new client acquisition
