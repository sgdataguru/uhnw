# User Story: 15 - Track Revenue Growth by Product Mix

**As a** Managing Partner,
**I want** to track revenue growth linked to product mix,
**so that** I can understand which products are driving growth and optimize the sales strategy.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Dashboard displays revenue breakdown by product category
* Growth rates shown for each product type
* Visual comparison of product contribution to total revenue
* Trend analysis over configurable time periods
* Ability to filter by RM, region, or client segment
* Highlights top-performing and underperforming products

## Notes

* Page 1 highlights: "Revenue growth tracking linked to product mix"
* Helps management understand product adoption and revenue drivers
* Supports strategic decision-making on product focus
