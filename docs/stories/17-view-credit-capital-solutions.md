# User Story: 17 - View Credit and Capital Solutions Visibility

**As a** Relationship Manager,
**I want** to see credit and capital solutions opportunities for my clients,
**so that** I can proactively offer relevant financing solutions during liquidity events.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Dashboard shows clients with potential credit/capital needs
* Displays existing credit facilities and utilization
* Highlights clients with upcoming capital needs based on signals
* Shows margin-related changes that may indicate financing needs
* Links credit opportunities to liquidity events
* Integration with lead scoring for prioritization

## Notes

* Page 1 highlights: "Credit/capital solutions visibility"
* Often tied to liquidity events and margin changes
* Proactive offering of solutions improves client engagement
